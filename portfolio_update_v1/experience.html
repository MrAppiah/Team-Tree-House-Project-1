Experience

My story in software development started when I was unsure of what I wanted to do with my life. Even though I had big goals and dreams I didn't know what career path was right for me. Long story short; I spoke to a stranger outside and he told me to do some research into software engineering. Furthermore, I used to get recommended videos on youtube on how people became self-taught software developers/engineers.

I have some experience before doing this course. I first went on Codecademy and used their free resources and then I purchased online courses on udemy. I now have 3 projects and a portfolio. Even though these courses have helped me, I don't think I've learnt a lot because the structure of the courses isn't as good as treehouse. Instead of being tested and challenged you just code alone and watch videos at the same time, it's not engaging and it doesn't suit my learning style. My plan is o learn all of the foundations again and improve my js skills, then learn a framework.
